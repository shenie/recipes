Roast lamb
==========

Ingredients
-----------

* lamb leg
* garlic
* rosemary
* lemon
* olive oil
* red wine
* salt
* pepper
* coriander
* potato
* tomato
* capsicum
* onion
* mushroom

Process
-------

1. Cut some slits on the upper side of lamb
2. insert some garlic and rosemary into each slit
3. 20 ~ 25 mins per 500g at 180c
